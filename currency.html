How to Pay in Mogadishu

Mogadishu runs largely on mobile money rather than cash or cards. The most widely used service is EVC Plus, operated by Hormuud Telecom — one of the most advanced mobile payment systems in the world. As a visitor, understanding how it works will make your trip much smoother.

1
Get a local SIM card — pick up a Hormuud Telecom SIM at the airport or any mobile shop in Mogadishu. This gives you access to EVC Plus immediately.
2
Load credit onto your SIM — you can top up at any Hormuud agent across the city. Just hand over cash and they load it to your number.
3
Pay by phone — dial *712# or use the EVC app to send money to shops, restaurants, hotels and taxis. Most businesses in Mogadishu accept EVC Plus.
4
Receive a confirmation SMS — every transaction sends an instant SMS confirmation to both parties, so you always have a record.
Visitor tip Always carry some Somali Shillings in cash as a backup, especially for smaller purchases at markets like Bakaara. Most hotels and restaurants also accept USD.
